oster.github.io

Öster is a social platform that matches you with professionals who are in your immediate vicinity. Whether from your field of interest, or in any field of interest you would like to explore, this app permits two individuals to meet, connect & network anytime, anywhere. Öster is a great way to have a nice lunch meeting or grab a cup of joe while building your network!
